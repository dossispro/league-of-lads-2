What is Lads?

League of Lads is a North American Dota 2 'beer league' for low immortals. It's a league for fun - we make memes and play games. We usually have about 16 teams who play over the course of 3 months to determine the Champion of that season.

League Structure

Make your own teams

The teams that play lads are self-assembled. You must find four other players and sign up as a team.

No set play days

We'll put out matches - three series (bo2) every two weeks - and it will be your responsibility to schedule with opposing teams.

Strict MMR allowances

We will only allow teams under our MMR average cap, and only allow players under MMR threshold. There's some allowance for returning players.
